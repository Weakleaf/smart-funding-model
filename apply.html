Submit a Band Appeal

If you believe your band placement does not reflect your financial situation, submit a formal appeal below.

! Before You Appeal
Appeals must be submitted within 30 days of band placement.
Provide your Application ID and a clear explanation of why you believe your band is incorrect. Supporting documents (income certificates, death certificates, disability cards) strengthen your case. Appeals are reviewed within 14 working days.
1 Appeal Details
Appeals are reviewed by the HELB Assessment Committee within 14 working days.
